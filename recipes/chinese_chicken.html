Chinese chicken

Description

Chinese chicken with pineapple!

My Mom's special recipe.

Ingredients

  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 carrot, chopped
  • 1 green pepper, chopped
  • 750g Chicken Pieces
  • 425g Pineapple pieces
  • 25ml Brown Vinegar
  • 12.5ml Sugar
  • 25ml Cornflour
  • 25ml Worcester Sauce
  • 50ml Chicken Stock
  • 12.5ml Soy Sauce

Steps

  1. Dry fry chicken til half cooked then put in a casserole dish
  2. Saute veges in chicken fat, then add pineapple & juice.
  3. Simmer 5 mins
  4. Mix Cornflour with remaining ingred. & stir into pan
  5. Bring to boil til thickens then pour over chicken
  6. Bake at 180 degrees (350 degree F) 1.5 hours
  7. Serve with rice or noodles
