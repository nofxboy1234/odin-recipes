Mum's Hamburgers

Description

Homemade Hamburgers

My Mom's special recipe.

Ingredients

  • 1 punnet mince
  • 1 chopped onion
  • 1 slice bread - crumbles
  • 2 eggs
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Aromat
  • Chutney
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Parsley

Steps

  1. Mix well, form into patties & put in fridge til ready to cook
  2. You can braai them or cook in a frying pan with BBQ spice sprinkled on top
  3. Serve on a roll with:
    • Lettuce
    • Cheese
    • Mayo
    • Chutney
    • T/Sauce
