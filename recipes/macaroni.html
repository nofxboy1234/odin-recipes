Macaroni

Description

Macaroni and cheese with bacon!

My Mom's special recipe.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 packet Bacon
  • 1 Onion
  • 1/2 chopped Green Pepper
  • 1 chopped Tomato
  • 1 cup chopped Mushrooms
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • 1 cup grated Cheese
  • Tomato Sauce

Steps

  1. Chop 1/2 packet Bacon & 1 onion.
  2. Microwave 4 mins
  3. Add 1/2 chopped Green Pepper
  4. Add 1 chopped Tomato
  5. Add 1 cup chopped Mushrooms
  6. Microwave for 2 mins
  7. Add salt, pepper & Aromat & cup grated cheese
  8. Mix
  9. Cook 1/2 pkt Macaroniin salt water - when soft - drain & mix into the veges
  10. Sprinkle cheese and tomato sauce on top
  11. Put in oven (180 degrees) for 1 hour
